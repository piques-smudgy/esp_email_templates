📝 Written exam (retake) sign-up – small upgrade
We have made a few improvements to the written exam sign-up workflow to make it clearer and more consistent.
  • Confirmation – successfully signed up
  • Date not found – please double-check the exam date
  • Too late – the two-week deadline has passed
  • Withdraw/Skip – indicate via the same form
Specific exams have a special deadline. Otherwise, the default two-week deadline applies. Upgrades for the practical retake sign-up will follow in a few weeks.
