ESP Students Update March '26 – wellbeing support, NSE updates, ESP25 and upcoming calendar.

Dear students,

We hope you are doing well. And to those observing Ramadan at the moment: we wish you a meaningful and peaceful period. Over the past weeks many of you may have been following news about conflicts in different parts of the world. As an international community with students from many countries, we realise that events abroad can have a real impact here as well — emotionally, personally or even financially.

If you are affected in any way and feel that this influences your wellbeing or studies, please know that you are not alone. Your study coach, the ESP team, and the AUAS support services are here to help. If financial circumstances have changed due to events in your home region, the AUAS counsellors and CSA can also provide guidance and possible financial lenience where needed.

Please do not hesitate to reach out to us or your study coach if you would like to talk or explore support options. We are here for you.
📊 NSE Update
A big thank you to Class 2028 for achieving an impressive 74% response rate in the National Student Survey (NSE). This is the highest response rate ESP has ever achieved and currently also the highest within our faculty — a fantastic result!

Classes 2027 and 2026: you are currently at 27%. We know you can do better and hope you can help us match the great example set by Class 2028. Higher response rates ensure that the results truly represent the student experience and help us improve the programme.

You can search your inbox for the original email with subject “Make your opinion count in the 2026 National Student Survey” (try searching for NSE in Outlook or Gmail). If you lost the link, you can retrieve it here:
Find my NSE link

Some of you may receive a short personal reminder with a personalised confirmation link. Because we cannot see the individual NSE responses ourselves, this link simply allows you to confirm that you already completed the survey. Once confirmed, you will not receive further reminders.
🎉 ESP is 25 – ticket sales opening soon
Our programme will celebrate its 25th anniversary in September 2026. Preparations are well underway and we expect to open ticket sales soon.

Stay tuned via our social channels — for example @espamsterdam on Instagram — and through the ESP LinkedIn page. Capacity will be limited, so once tickets go live it will be first come, first served.

While you are on LinkedIn, please also make sure ESP appears in your profile so our community becomes more visible online. #espamsterdam
